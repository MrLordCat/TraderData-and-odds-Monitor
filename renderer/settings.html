Hotkeys

  • Tab — switch between Brokers and Stats
  • F1 — toggle Auto (works while the app window is active)
  • F2 — Auto Resume after Suspend (toggle)
  • F3 — start/stop Excel/Python script
  • Num5 — global (even outside the app) toggle Auto
Tab/F1/F2/F3 are handled only when the app is active. Num5 is a global hotkey (if registered with the OS).
